Japanese Beef Curry

Description

Japanese beef curry is one of my most favorite comfort foods out there. Soft tender beef coated in rich flavorful curry over rice. Simple to make and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Curry Roux
  • Oil/Butter
  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Potatoes (Optional)
  • Carrots(Optional)

Steps

  1. Add 2tbsp of oil or butter into heated pot
  2. Brown all sides of cubed beef in a pot and then remove to set aside
  3. Add sliced onions and lightly brown
  4. Add beef back in along with potatoes and carrots if you have those
  5. Add water and bring to boil then reduce to simmer
  6. Cook rice during this time
  7. After simmering for 25 minutes, add curry roux
  8. Mix well and serve over rice
