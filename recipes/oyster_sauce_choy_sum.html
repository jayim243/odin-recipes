Oyster Sauce Choy Sum

Description

Oyster sauce choy sum is one of the simplest vegetable dishes to make out there and it requires very little ingredients. All you really need is choy sum, oyster sauce, and garlic. You can definitely add extra seasoning and spices to your liking. Simple yet delicious and healthy dish.

Ingredients

  • Choy Sum
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Corn Starch Slurry

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. Blanch choy sum in boiling water for 1 minute to 2 minutes. The longer you blanch, the softer the choy sum will be
  3. Remove choy sum and strain as much water off as possible, then set on a plate
  4. In a sauce pan, add oil, garlic, oyster sauce, corn starch slurry and mix it
  5. Heat up the sauce pan for about 30 seconds or until the sauce starts thickening
  6. Pour sauce over choy sum and serve
